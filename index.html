Jiahui (Kent) Huang's webpage


I am a PhD student at the University of Waterloo under Prof. Matthew Satriano.
Email: j346huan@uwaterloo.ca
I study algebraic geometry and motivic integration, and focus mainly on the applications of arc spaces to singularity theory. Recently, I've am working on a construction of a new cohomology theory for Artin stacks.
